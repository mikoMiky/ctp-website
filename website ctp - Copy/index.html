Introduction

About Changloon Transformation Project

Changloon, a town with unique cultural heritage, is at a turning point. The Changloon Transformation Project Framework is an official government initiative that aims to guide the town toward becoming a knowledge society with a forward-thinking mindset.

This strategic framework targets waste management, tourism, knowledge, and social transformation. The goal is to foster a sustainable, informed, and unified community in alignment with national development goals and the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).

View Official Framework

Vision & Mission

V

Vision

Transform Changloon into a leading knowledge society, driving innovation, sustainability, and community empowerment through practical solutions and the living lab approach. Align efforts with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) to create a resilient and thriving town.

M

Mission

To implement effective transformation across waste management, tourism, knowledge, and social sectors through community engagement, education, and sustainable practices. We aim to create a model of development that can be replicated in other communities.
